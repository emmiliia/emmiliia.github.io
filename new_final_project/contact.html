Issue N°2 is a monthly online magazine focusing on culinary creations. Each chapter contains several articles from interviews to recipes.

I'm a big fan of Netflix and love the fact that these days I'm able to watch a whole season of my favorite series at once. That's why I wanted to give my readers the opportunity to have access to a bunch of new articles the same way.

I used to write a health blog called in Finnish. My gluten free recipes have been captured in two cook books sold in Finland. Issue N2 is my second website hence the name. This time I felt the need to create more than a blog. Being a journalist it was clear that I wanted to write more interviews and feature articles.

I've taken photographs since I was a teenager. I got into digital photography quite late because I loved film so much. I spent most of high school in a dark room.

These days I sit on my computer far too often. When I don't, I dance ballet, cook and listen to my favorite songs on repeat.
