Naturally amazing

Not quite the typical recipe book, Aidosti kaunis (Genuine beauty), serves you fresh ideas on home made skin care. Katja Kokko is a Finnish beauty blogger who specifies in natural cosmetics. Kaapo Kamu is a photographer behind calm imagenary.

At the moment the book is only available in Finnish but Katja told me that the international edition is on its way. In the meantime, you can visit her blog for further inspiration.

Aidosti kaunis, xx €. Cozy Publishing

Take me back

Ever smelled something that took you back in time? Finnish lifestyle brand Samuji's hand made soaps are impossible to ignore while at the cashier. The scents are intoxicating yet very elegant. Ah, heck! What's another 10 euros when you are already about to spend hundreds on a warm merino wool jumper. The birch and vetiver soap is a reminder of ”warm” Finnish summer nights. Or want to pretend you finally started that herb garden you've been talking about the last three years? Rosemary is your choice.

Samuji, 10 € each.

Psst! The soaps are not currently available at the webshop. Another good reason for a vacation in Helsinki!

Spoonful of beauty

These unique spoons are all one could ever want in their kitchen. At least if you already own a Vitamix blender and an impressive china collection. Organise your superfoods in pretty jars and keep this wooden beauty around. Making smoothies has never looked so good. The spoons are made out of olive tree, hence the cool pattern.

Samuji, 15 €.
