About

I'm Emmi-Liia Sjöholm, a 30-year-old Helsinki based journalist who specializes in text, visuals and recipes.

Say hello and work with me!

Text

I started my journalistic career in 2007 as a news reporter in Helsingin Sanomat, Finland’s biggest newspaper. Since then I’ve worked for several magazined (Trendi, Cosmopolitan, Kauppalehti and MeNaiset just to name a few) and other publications.

Besides media, I also work with other clients. For example I’ve created a seasonal online publication for a lifestyle store.

Say hello and work with me!

Visuals

I’ve been taking photographs since I was a teenager. Eventually film and dark rooms got replaced by piles of plates, spices and a digital camera. I’ve taken photographs for two of my cook books as well as for my blogs.

I’ve also worked closely with art directors and professional photographers in different publications.

Say hello and work with me!

Recipes

I specialize in gluten-free and wholesome food. Besides making recipes for my own sites and books, I’ve worked with clients.

Say hello and work with me!

Books

Gluten-free and simple – my books offer wholesome cooking ideas. My first book ”Viljaton kattaus” was published in January of 2015 and my second book ”Koko perheen kattaus” a year later. They are both packed with over 90 gluten-free recipes but ”Koko perheen kattaus” is also dairy-free and egg-free and its recipes are made with kids and families in mind. Both books are available in Finnish only.
